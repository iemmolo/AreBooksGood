## Key Design Decisions
- Hybrid combat: manual + auto toggle per pet
- Level caps: Common 30→50, Rare 25→50→75, Legendary 50→75→99
- 4 equipped moves from 8-12 learned pool
- Full spec trees: DPS(Assassin/Berserker), Tank(Juggernaut/Paladin), Support(Healer/Buffer)
- Variable party size: 1-4 per side
- Move management in pet detail popup (side panel)
- Training Arena → 1v1 arena battles (replaces click mini-game)
- Dungeon Gate → dungeon select modal → team builder → battle
- New file: rpg-combat.js, rpg-combat.css
- Data files: rpg-moves.json, rpg-dungeons.json, rpg-spec-trees.json
- Storage: rpg-slot-N-combat (per-slot, isolated)
- Stat formula: diminishing growth (8% Lv1-30, 4% Lv31-60, 2% Lv61-99)
- Legacy battle.js UNTOUCHED
