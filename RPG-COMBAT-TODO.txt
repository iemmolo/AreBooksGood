## Key Design Decisions
- Hybrid combat: manual + auto toggle per pet (currently per-battle, goal: per-pet)
- Level caps: Common 30→50, Rare 25→50→75, Legendary 50→75→99
- 4 equipped moves from learned pool (all type-matching moves up to current level)
- Equipment buffs: lead pet 100%, others 50% of player equip ATK/DEF
- Training Arena = rpg-combat.js 1v1 (old click-mini-game stays on standalone /pets/skills/)
- Spec-gated moves: 16 total (2 per gate), covers all 9 spec gates
- Special move mechanics: recklessCharge (recoil), bloodFrenzy (self-damage), divineSmite (self-regen), holyShield (AoE shield)
- Legacy battle.js UNTOUCHED
- Files: rpg-combat.js, rpg-combat.css, rpg-moves.json, rpg-dungeons.json
- Storage: rpg-slot-N-combat (per-slot, isolated)
